Privacy Policy

OtaQuiz is a private content creation tool used to publish quiz videos to TikTok.

The application only accesses the TikTok account of the authenticated user through TikTok OAuth authentication.

No personal data is collected, stored, shared, or sold to third parties.

Access tokens are used solely to publish content on behalf of the authorized user.

Users can revoke access to the application at any time via their TikTok account settings.
